
# Silence
Silence is a LSPosed module that allows you to freeze or kill apps when they are idle or in your freeze policy, preventing them from running in the background and consuming resources.  

It is designed to be simple and easy to use, and provide multiple customization options to suit your needs and fit your devices.

## Supported
### Freeze Types
- **cgroup v2** - supported
- **kill -19 (SIGSTOP)** - planned
### Tested Devices
- **HyperOS3.0**: Kernel 5.15 - Android 16

<br>
<br>
<br>

## How to use
1. Install Silence
2. Open LSPosed to enable Silence in **system**
3. Reboot your device
4. Open Silence
